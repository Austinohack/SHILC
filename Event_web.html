A Year of Global Opportunities: Our School Joins the ACE Regional Student Convention in Thailand and the International Student Convention in the USA!

We are proud to announce that our students will be representing our school at two prestigious events this year: the Annual ACE Regional Student Convention in Thailand and the International Student Convention in the USA. These events offer our students an exceptional opportunity to compete on a global stage, showcase their talents, and engage with peers from around the world.

RSC

ACE Regional Student Convention in Thailand

In September, our students will travel to Thailand to participate in the ACE Regional Student Convention. This event gathers students from across the region to compete in various categories, including academics, arts, athletics, and leadership. The convention not only serves as a competition but also as a platform for cultural exchange, where students can learn about different traditions, form new friendships, and broaden their horizons.

Highlights of the Convention:

•Academic Challenges: Testing students’ knowledge in key subjects like mathematics, science, and history.

•Creative Arts Competitions: Opportunities to showcase talents in music, drama, visual arts, and more.

•Sports and Athletics: Competing in team sports, track and field events, and physical fitness challenges.

•Leadership Events: Demonstrating leadership and communication skills through speeches, debates, and service projects.

JSC

The Saigon Heritage International Learning Center recently became a part of the Accelerated Christian Education (ACE) Junior Student Convention. At the ACE Junior Student Convention, the Saigon Heritage International Learning Center participated in different events such as platform, music, arts, sports and academic.

ISC

International Student Convention in the USA

Following the regional event in Thailand, our top-performing students will have the honor of advancing to the International Student Convention in the USA, scheduled for next spring. This global event brings together students from around the world to compete at the highest level and celebrate their achievements. It’s a unique opportunity for our students to engage with a diverse international community and gain exposure to different educational and cultural perspectives.

What to Expect:

Global Competitions: Compete with the best students from around the world in various categories.

Cultural Exchange: Experience American culture and connect with students from different countries.

Inspiring Keynote Speakers: Learn from world-class educators and leaders who will share their insights and experiences.

Exploration and Adventure: Take part in excursions and activities that highlight the beauty and history of the host country.
