Fun and Fellowship: Exciting School Activities at Saigon Heritage International Learning Center

At Saigon Heritage International Learning Center, we believe that education extends beyond the classroom. Our diverse range of activities, such as bowling, billiards, and picnics, provide students with opportunities to relax, have fun, and build strong relationships with their peers and teachers.

Bowling

Bowling: Striking Up Fun

Bowling is one of our most popular activities, offering students a fun and engaging way to unwind after a busy week of classes. Whether they’re seasoned players or trying bowling for the first time, students enjoy the friendly competition and camaraderie that comes with hitting the lanes. It’s a great way for them to develop teamwork skills, hand-eye coordination, and, most importantly, have a blast with their friends.

Billiard

Billiards: A Game of Skill and Strategy

Our students also have the opportunity to try their hand at billiards, a game that combines skill, strategy, and concentration. Playing billiards helps students develop critical thinking and precision, as they plan their shots and strategize their moves. It’s a relaxing yet challenging activity that students love, and it’s another way for them to connect with their peers in a fun, social environment.

Picnic

Picnics: Enjoying the Great Outdoors

Few things bring people together like a good old-fashioned picnic. Our school organizes regular picnic outings to beautiful parks and scenic locations around Ho Chi Minh City. These picnics are a chance for students to enjoy the outdoors, share a meal, and participate in various recreational activities, from playing games to simply lounging and enjoying the fresh air. It’s a perfect way to bond with classmates and teachers in a relaxed, informal setting.
